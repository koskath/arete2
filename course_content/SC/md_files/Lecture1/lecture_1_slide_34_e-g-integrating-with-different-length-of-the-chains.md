In this context, customers or end users trigger the order flow, which connects to suppliers or vendors, purchasing and forecasting, sourcing, procurement and production, the fulfilment flow and finally the payment flow.

There can be different lengths of supply chains: (1) “short”, delivered from stock – S, (2) “medium”, made to order – S and (3) “long”, engineered to order – S. The challenge is how to determine the “optimal complexity” and the optimal “customer penetration point”.
