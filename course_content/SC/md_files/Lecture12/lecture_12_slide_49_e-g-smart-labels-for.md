# e.g. Smart Labels for
Applications in Retail and Logistics
 Smart labels for open loop applications are on the market since the
late 90s
 Reading ranges today up to 12 meters (depending a lot on
transmission frequencies)
 Costs per tag less than a few €Cents
 Functions:
−
Identification of products, packaging and transportation means
like skeleton boxes, pallets
−
Storage of additional data (ID plus 512 bit)
−
Bulk reading up to 500 tags in a few seconds
−
Locating possible with special infrastructure
