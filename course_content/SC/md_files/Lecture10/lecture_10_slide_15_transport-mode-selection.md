# Transport Mode Selection

A function of:
 Weight/volume and value of the freight
 Distance travelled
 Availability / reliability of transport services
 Freight rates to be charged
 ...
Instead of tare weight, carriers alternatively apply volumetric charging
based on consignment dimensions (usually abbreviated by W/M):
 Road/rail 1:3 or 1 cbm = 333 kg
 Sea freight 1:1 or 1 cbm = 1,000 kg
 Air freight 1:6 or 1 cbm = 666 kg
Always the higher one is taken!
