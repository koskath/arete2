What is logistics management?
“Logistics management is that part of Supply Chain Management that
plans, implements, and controls the efficient, effective forward and
reverse flow and storage of goods, services, and related
information between the point of origin and the point of
consumption in order to meet customer requirements.”
CLM definition of Logistics Management (2003) as quoted in Mentzer et al. (2004)
