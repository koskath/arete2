# „Connecting the dots“ of a Supply Chain
This slide is about “connecting the dots” of a supply chain and asks what is typically mapped. It refers to Srai and Gregory’s work on a supply network configuration perspective on international supply chain development, highlighting how supply network structures are represented and analysed.

Srai, Jagit Singh; Gregory, Mike: A supply network configuration perspective on international supply chain development, in: International Journal of Operations & Production Management, 28(2008)5, pp. 386–411
