# … – Footloose Industries and the “Goose
Flight”
Continuing the theme of “footloose” industries and the “goose flight”, this slide asks who produces garments in Asia. Over time, production has shifted between countries such as Japan, Taiwan, ASEAN countries, China, Vietnam, India, Kazakhstan and others, illustrating how competitive advantage in certain industries migrates across regions.

Quelle: Kwan, Chi; NRI Spring97, S. 58 ff.
