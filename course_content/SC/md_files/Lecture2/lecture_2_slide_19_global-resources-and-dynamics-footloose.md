# Global Resources and Dynamics – Footloose
Industries and the “Goose Flight”
This slide introduces the idea of “footloose” industries and the “goose flight” pattern in global resource allocation and industrial dynamics. It shows how the competitive edge of a country such as Japan shifts over time across different industries, ranging from textiles and chemicals to iron and steel, autos and electronics.

Quelle: Kwan, Chi; NRI Spring97, S. 58 ff.
